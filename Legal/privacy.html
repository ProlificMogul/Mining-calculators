Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Mining Calculators. I, Munyaradzi Mutyaso, respect your privacy and am committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how I look after your personal data when you visit this website and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

Information We Collect

When you use Mining Calculators, I may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Information on how you use the website, which calculators you access, and how you interact with the Mining AI Assistant.
  • Calculator Inputs: Data you enter into the calculators to perform calculations (e.g., mineral deposit parameters, economic factors, production rates).
  • Account Information: If you create an account, I collect your email address and password (stored securely).
  • Contact Information: If you use the contact form, I collect your name and email address.

How We Use Your Information

I use the information I collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the calculators and Mining AI Assistant.
  • To improve the functionality and user experience of the website.
  • To remember your preferences and calculation history if you create an account.
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve the tools.

Data Storage and Security

Calculator inputs are processed in your browser and are not stored on the server unless you explicitly save them to your account. If you choose to save calculations, this data is stored securely with appropriate technical measures to prevent unauthorized access.

Account information is securely stored and encrypted. I implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data against accidental loss, unauthorized access, or disclosure.

Mining AI Assistant

The Mining AI Assistant processes your queries to provide relevant responses. While queries may be temporarily stored to improve the AI system, they are not linked to personally identifiable information unless you are logged into an account.

The AI Assistant does not use your data for purposes other than providing mining-related assistance and improving its capabilities.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit the website. They help to provide you with a better experience by remembering your preferences and enabling certain functionality.

You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, but this may limit your ability to use some features of the website.

Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services for analytics, hosting, and other functions. These services may collect and process data on my behalf in accordance with their privacy policies.

Links to external websites are provided for convenience. I am not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access the personal data I hold about you.
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • The right to request deletion of your personal data.
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time where I am relying on consent to process your personal data.
  • The right to request restriction of processing of your personal data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me using the contact form on the website.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. The most current version will always be posted on this page, with the effective date updated accordingly.

Significant changes will be communicated through an update notification on the website.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or my data practices, please contact me through the contact form on the website.
